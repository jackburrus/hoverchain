Welcome to HoverChain

HoverChain is a Chrome extension that enhances your browsing experience by providing interactive insights into Ethereum addresses and smart contracts you encounter online. Simply select any Ethereum address to reveal a tooltip with options for deeper exploration.

How does it work?

Once you set your api key in the toolbar popup ☝️, HoverChain does all the heavy lifing right in your browser to get you all the up to date blockchain information.

Try selecting this Ethereum wallet address to see its associated NFTs, transfers, and tokens: 0x34aA3F359A9D614239015126635CE7732c18fDF3.

Why is it useful?

Context switching is a massive drain on our focus. With HoverChain, you never have to leave the context of the information you are interacting with in order to learn more about a wallet or contract.

Wallets and Contracts are links to troves of data, yet we don't always provide a full link to Etherscan or Opensea. HoverChain does the link aggregation for you.
